Cheese Roll

Description

These cheese rolls are heavenly. I first tried the cheese rolls at Mary Grace. Although they were delicious, I thought they were too expensive. So, I decided to make them at home. It's not that tough! Recipe adapted from Foxy Folsky.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1 cup milk lukewarm
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 1 stick unsalted butter or shortening

Topping

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter softened
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar

Filling

  • 16 pieces cheese sticks 3-inch long and 1cm thick

Procedure

Water Roux

  1. In a small pot, cook the bread flour with water until it is thicken.
  2. Cover and set aside.

Bread Dough

  1. Mix all dry ingredients for the bread in the Kitchen Aid mixing bowl.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk egg together with water.

Coffee Topping

  1. Dissolve the coffee powder in hot water and then add vanilla essence.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients for the bread in the Kitchen Aid mixing bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk egg together with water.

Filling

  1. Stir all ingredients for the fillng.
